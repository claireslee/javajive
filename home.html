Our Story

Nestled in the heart of the city, Java Jive is more than just a coffee shop - it's a celebration of craft coffee culture and community. Since 2015, we've been carefully sourcing and roasting our own beans, creating unique blends that tell stories through every sip. Our passionate baristas are dedicated to the art of coffee-making, ensuring each cup is crafted to perfection. Whether you're starting your morning routine, meeting friends, or seeking a cozy spot to work, Java Jive offers a warm welcome and an exceptional coffee experience.

Why Choose Java Jive?

  • Ethically sourced beans from small-scale farmers, ensuring fair trade practices and sustainable agriculture
  • In-house roasting that guarantees freshness and allows us to create signature flavor profiles
  • Award-winning baristas who craft each drink with precision and artistic flair
  • Community-focused space that hosts local events, showcases local artists, and gives back to neighborhood initiatives
